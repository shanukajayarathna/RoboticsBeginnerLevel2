# Level 1 lesson images — drop-in folder

Put photos/screenshots here and tell Claude "images are in images/l1/".
Claude will wire each into the right lesson and push.

**Guidelines**
- Format: JPG (photos) or PNG (screenshots).
- Web-size them first: max ~1600px wide, ideally under ~500 KB each, so pages load fast.
- Use your OWN photos where possible (your kit, your class) — they're the most engaging and you own the rights. Manufacturer product photos are fine for hardware.
